About US

We are a group of five Computer Science students at the University of Massachusetts, Amherst. We like understanding technologies that are at the forefront of computer science research. And, obviously, we like doing things at WEBSCALE.

This project is part of a semester-long assignment for the class COMPSCI 326 (Web Programming) with Tim Richards. This is an opportunity to create a web-based project completely by our own hands and using our own minds.
